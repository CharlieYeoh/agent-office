IB Theory of Knowledge – Assessment Criteria
=============================================

Add the official IB TOK essay / exhibition criteria here.

TOK Essay Criteria (out of 10 each):
- Criterion A: Understanding Knowledge Questions
- Criterion B: Knowledge Framework Application
- Criterion C: Use of Examples
- Criterion D: Structure & Argument
- Criterion E: Effective Language Use

Exhibition Criteria (out of 10 each):
- Criterion A: Exhibition Objects & Justification
- Criterion B: Connection to TOK Theme / Core Theme
- Criterion C: Quality of Analysis
